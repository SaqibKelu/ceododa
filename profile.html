About the Office

Chief Education Office, Doda

Established in 1942 as the District Education Office and renamed the Chief Education Office (CEO) Doda in 1993, the Office of the Chief Education Officer, Doda is the apex district-level authority responsible for the planning, implementation, supervision, and monitoring of school education across the district.

The office works in close coordination with the Directorate of School Education, Jammu, and other allied departments to ensure equitable, inclusive, and quality education for every child. At present, it oversees a vast network of 1,133 government schools and 271 private schools, implementing educational policies and initiatives of both the State and Central Governments. The mandate of the Chief Education Office, Doda spans academic administration, teacher management, infrastructure development, student welfare schemes, digital initiatives, and community engagement, with special focus on remote and underserved areas. The office ensures effective policy execution through timely dissemination of guidelines, institutional support, and continuous progress monitoring. In addition, the office manages resource allocation, including funds and infrastructure projects, to strengthen the education system and enhance learning environments. It also plays a key role in the professional development of teachers and staff through training programmes, workshops, and seminars aimed at improving teaching standards and enriching classroom practices.

Through evidence-based planning, transparent governance, and continuous monitoring, the Chief Education Office, Doda serves as a vital platform for addressing educational challenges and fostering positive change. It remains central to the district’s educational framework, committed to improving learning outcomes and ensuring access to quality education for every child, thereby shaping the future of coming generations.

Flagship Digital Initiative

Record Management System (RMS)

A secure, role-based digital platform that digitizes, manages, and provides controlled access to departmental records — including public-facing documents like orders, circulars, and notifications.

Complete Digitization & Archiving

Seamless upload, scanning, and indexing of legacy records with full-text search and long-term digital preservation.

Role-Based Secure Login

Multi-level access control with audit logs, ensuring internal security and compliance.

Public Document Portal

Citizens and stakeholders can securely view, search, and download orders, circulars, and notifications online.

Analytics & Monitoring Dashboard

Real-time insights into record usage, digitization progress, and access patterns for efficient administration.

Integrated Governance Platform

Integrated Management System (IMS)

A unified, all-in-one platform combining School, Employee, Student, Attendance, and Budget Management — delivering real-time, department-wide administrative control in a single secure system.

School Management System

Complete oversight of infrastructure, enrollment, academic performance, and compliance across all institutions.

Employee & Student Management

Centralized HR and student records with biometric attendance, leave tracking, and real-time status updates.

Attendance & Leave Automation

Budget & Financial Reporting

End-to-end budget planning, expenditure tracking, and dynamic MIS reports for audit-ready transparency.
